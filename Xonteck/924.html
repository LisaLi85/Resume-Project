  • Model 924 Toxic Air Sampler

  • The Xonteck Model 924 Toxic Air Sampler is designed for unattended field use to collect ambient air samples for laboratory analysis of toxic compounds.
  • The sampler is modular in design and consists of a control unit, pump box assembly, rain shield, and sampling head mount. The sampler can accept from one to four sampling channels that include 37/47mm filter for Cr6 and other particulates, Carbonyl with optional pre-filter, Sorbent tube with upstream actuation valve, and Canister (VOC).*

  • Features

  • ● Canister, Carbonyl, 37/47mm Filter (Cr6), and Sorbent tube multi- configurable

    ● Weather proof four-channel stand-alone sampling station

    ● Corrosion resistant stainless steel and brass fittings

    ● Integrated computer with printer and touch-screen display

    ● Temperature controlled heater and fans for cold or hot weather operation

    ● Modular design for ease of assembly, installation, operation and service

  • Specifications

    Sample Flow Control: Porter Model 201, 202 Mass Flow Controller, typically 0-2SLPM, 0-30SLPM or customer specified, accuracy and linearity to ±1% of full scale.

    Display: touch screen, TFT color graphics, white LED backlight, 5.7” diagonal, 320 x 240 pixel density.

    External Sample Pump: Gast 1023 Series Motor Mounted Rotary Vane, max vacuum 26” Hg, open flow 10.0 CFM.

    Digital Vacuum Transducers: -30”Hg to 0psig, ±0.5 % of Full Scale (BFSL).

    Integrated Temperature Control: Designed into PC circuit for enclosure heat control via 100 Watt silicone rubber heater.

    Temperature Display: enclosure temperature displayed, Honeywell RTD sensor, -40 °C to 150 °C, 1 °C resolution.

    Power: 115V/60Hz; 220V/50Hz(optional).

    Dimensions: 7’(H)×1’10”(W) ×1’(D)

    Weights:
    Sampler (pump not included): 128lbs
    Sample Pump: 52lbs

  • The sampler can accept any combination of sampling channels with the exception of the canister channel. The sampler can only accept one canister channel.
